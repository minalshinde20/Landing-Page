It's not just coffee
It's Starbucks

Starbucks coffee is renowned for its high-quality, ethically sourced beans and diverse range of blends and roasts. The company sources its coffee from the top 3% of coffee beans available, primarily from Latin America, Africa, and Asia-Pacific regions. Starbucks is committed to ethical sourcing practices, working closely with farmers through its Coffee and Farmer Equity (C.A.F.E.) Practices to ensure fair trade and sustainable farming methods. The brand offers a variety of coffee options, including whole bean, ground, and instant coffee, along with espresso-based drinks like lattes, cappuccinos, and macchiatos. Their signature blends, such as Pike Place Roast and the bold Italian Roast, cater to different flavor preferences. Starbucks continually innovates with seasonal and limited-edition blends, such as the popular Pumpkin Spice Latte. In addition to hot coffee, they provide cold brew, nitro cold brew, and iced coffee, expanding their appeal to a broader audience. Through meticulous attention to quality and ethical practices, Starbucks has become a global coffee leader, shaping coffee culture worldwide.
